Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Superb Executive Transportation respects customer privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Superb Executive Transportation collects, uses, stores, and shares information submitted through this website, including the booking-request page, contact forms, EmailJS or other form/email submission services, email links, phone links, and related service-request communications.

This website is used to receive transportation-service requests. Submitting information through the website does not create an account, does not guarantee service availability, and does not automatically confirm a reservation.

1. Information We Collect

Superb Executive Transportation may collect information that you submit when requesting transportation service, contacting the business, or communicating about a trip, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Pickup location
  • Drop-off location
  • Travel date and time
  • Passenger count
  • Service type
  • Round-trip or return-service details
  • Flight, ferry, arrival, departure, airline, vessel, or travel-coordination details
  • Ferry, airport, hotel, villa, private residence, or event-related trip details
  • Special instructions or notes you choose to provide
  • Any other information you voluntarily submit through the website or related communications

The website or related service providers may also collect limited technical information, such as device type, browser type, IP address, general usage information, form-submission data, and basic website-performance information.

2. How We Use Information

Superb Executive Transportation uses collected information to:

  • Receive and review booking requests
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Confirm trip details
  • Determine service availability
  • Coordinate pickup, drop-off, timing, route, and passenger details
  • Communicate by phone, text message, or email about a requested service
  • Provide quotes, deposit instructions, payment instructions, receipts, or service updates
  • Maintain business, accounting, tax, customer-service, and operational records
  • Improve the website, booking process, and customer experience
  • Protect the business, customers, website, and booking process from fraud, abuse, unauthorized activity, or misuse

3. Booking Requests Are Not Automatic Confirmations

A booking request submitted through the website is a request for service only. Service is not confirmed until Superb Executive Transportation reviews the request and confirms availability, pricing, deposit requirements, pickup details, and final service arrangements.

St. John-related service requests, ferry-related transfers, special-event service, multi-stop service, and other coordinated transportation requests may require additional review before confirmation.

4. Communications

By submitting a booking request, contact form, or service inquiry, you authorize Superb Executive Transportation to contact you using the phone number, email address, or other contact information you provide. Communications may include booking follow-up, trip coordination, pricing, deposit instructions, payment instructions, service updates, customer-service messages, calls, text messages, or emails related to your requested transportation service.

Message and data rates may apply. Customers may request that Superb Executive Transportation stop sending nonessential communications, but service-related communications may still be necessary to review, coordinate, confirm, update, or complete a requested transportation service.

Customers should provide accurate contact information. Superb Executive Transportation is not responsible for delays or failed communications caused by incorrect, incomplete, blocked, or unavailable contact information.

5. Payment Information

The website booking form is not intended to collect full payment-card numbers, bank-account numbers, Social Security numbers, passport numbers, or other highly sensitive financial or identification information.

Customers should not place credit card numbers, bank information, government identification numbers, or other sensitive personal information in open text fields.

Payment, deposit, and balance arrangements may be handled separately through approved payment methods, invoices, payment links, or third-party payment processors. Any third-party payment processor may have its own privacy policy and terms.

6. Sharing of Information

Superb Executive Transportation does not sell customer personal information.

Superb Executive Transportation may share information only when reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide or coordinate requested transportation services
  • Use website hosting, form, email, communication, booking, payment, accounting, or technology-service providers
  • Process payments, deposits, receipts, refunds, or accounting records
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, tax, insurance, or business-record obligations
  • Protect the rights, safety, property, customers, operations, or legal interests of Superb Executive Transportation
  • Respond to lawful requests from courts, law enforcement, government agencies, insurers, or legal representatives

7. Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services for hosting, form submission, email delivery, website operation, analytics, payment processing, or customer communication. These services may include EmailJS or other form/email submission providers used to transmit booking requests and related customer communications. These providers may process information as needed to operate the website, transmit booking requests, deliver messages, process payments, or support business operations.

Superb Executive Transportation is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites, platforms, processors, or services that are not controlled by Superb Executive Transportation.

8. Data Retention

Superb Executive Transportation may retain booking requests, contact information, communications, payment-related records, and service records for as long as reasonably necessary for business operations, customer service, accounting, tax, insurance, legal, dispute-resolution, and recordkeeping purposes.

Information that is no longer reasonably needed may be deleted, archived, or anonymized at the discretion of Superb Executive Transportation, subject to legal, accounting, tax, insurance, and operational requirements.

9. Security

Superb Executive Transportation takes reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the website and related communication channels. However, no website, email system, form service, payment processor, or internet transmission method is completely secure.

Customers should avoid submitting sensitive financial, identification, medical, confidential, or unnecessary personal information through website forms, email, or open text fields.

10. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for adults requesting transportation services. Superb Executive Transportation does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the website.

11. Customer Requests

Customers may contact Superb Executive Transportation to request correction, update, or deletion of contact information submitted through the website. Requests may be limited by business, legal, accounting, tax, insurance, dispute-resolution, and recordkeeping obligations.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Superb Executive Transportation may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
