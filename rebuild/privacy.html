1. Information We Collect

Superb Executive Transportation may collect information that customers provide when requesting, confirming, or using transportation services.

This may include:

  • name
  • phone number
  • email address
  • pickup and drop-off locations
  • service date and time
  • passenger count
  • flight, ferry, hotel, villa, or travel information
  • luggage, accessibility, or special assistance details
  • payment-related confirmation information
  • messages, requests, notes, or other information submitted by the customer

2. How We Collect Information

Information may be collected when a customer submits a booking form, sends an email, makes a phone call, sends a text message, confirms a reservation, pays a deposit, requests a quote, asks a question, or communicates with Superb Executive Transportation before, during, or after service.

3. How We Use Information

Superb Executive Transportation uses customer information only for transportation-related business purposes.

This includes:

  • responding to booking requests
  • confirming reservations
  • coordinating pickup and drop-off details
  • communicating about delays, schedule changes, payment, or service issues
  • providing transportation services
  • handling customer questions, disputes, cancellations, no-shows, damages, or lost property
  • maintaining business records
  • complying with legal, insurance, safety, tax, or operational requirements

4. Booking Form and Email Handling

Information submitted through the website booking form may be transmitted by email or through an email service provider used to deliver booking requests and customer communications.

Superb Executive Transportation uses this information to receive the customer’s request, respond to the customer, coordinate service, and maintain reservation records. Customers should not submit sensitive personal information through the booking form unless it is necessary for the transportation request.

5. Payments

Superb Executive Transportation may collect or receive payment-related information needed to confirm deposits, balances, refunds, billing issues, or payment disputes. Payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers depending on the payment method used.

Customers should follow the payment instructions provided at booking or confirmation and should not send unnecessary sensitive financial information by email, text message, or website form.

6. Information Sharing

Superb Executive Transportation does not sell customer personal information.

Customer information may be shared only when necessary to operate the business, provide service, process communications, handle payment-related issues, comply with legal obligations, respond to safety concerns, protect rights or property, or work with service providers that support the website, email, payment, booking, or business operations.

7. Customer Photos, Names, and Trip Details

Superb Executive Transportation will not use a customer’s name, photo, image, trip details, booking information, or private travel details for marketing or promotional purposes without permission.

8. Data Retention

Superb Executive Transportation keeps customer information only as long as reasonably needed for booking records, customer communication, accounting, tax, legal, insurance, safety, dispute-resolution, and business purposes.

Information may be deleted or archived when it is no longer needed, unless retention is required for lawful business, tax, insurance, safety, or legal reasons.

9. Security

Superb Executive Transportation takes reasonable steps to protect customer information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no website, email system, text message, electronic transmission, or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Customers should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information through unsecured communication methods.

10. Third-Party Services

The website and booking process may rely on third-party services for email delivery, website hosting, payment processing, analytics, maps, links, or other business functions. These services may process information according to their own privacy practices and terms.

Superb Executive Transportation is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites, platforms, payment providers, or services that are not controlled by Superb Executive Transportation.

11. Website Links

The website may contain links to third-party websites or services. A link does not mean Superb Executive Transportation controls, endorses, or is responsible for that third-party website, service, privacy policy, or content.

12. Children and Minors

Superb Executive Transportation does not knowingly collect personal information directly from minors for marketing purposes. Transportation involving minors must be arranged by a responsible adult, and minors are not permitted to ride unless accompanied by a responsible adult as stated in the Terms and Conditions.

13. Customer Choices and Requests

Customers may contact Superb Executive Transportation to request correction, update, or deletion of personal information, subject to business, legal, tax, insurance, safety, accounting, dispute-resolution, and recordkeeping needs.

Superb Executive Transportation may need to retain certain information when necessary to document reservations, payments, cancellations, no-shows, damages, disputes, safety issues, or legal compliance.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Superb Executive Transportation may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

15. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to Superb Executive Transportation.
