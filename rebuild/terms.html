1. Booking Requests and Reservations

Submitting a booking request does not automatically confirm service. A reservation is confirmed only after Superb Executive Transportation accepts the booking in writing and receives the required deposit or payment.

A website form submission, text message, email inquiry, phone call, or quote request does not create a confirmed reservation unless Superb Executive Transportation provides written confirmation.

Superb Executive Transportation may decline any booking request when the requested service, route, time, passenger count, conduct risk, or vehicle availability does not fit available service capacity.

2. Service Area and Applicable Law

Superb Executive Transportation provides transportation services in the U.S. Virgin Islands. These Terms and Conditions are governed by applicable Virgin Islands law.

3. Passenger Limit

Service is limited to a maximum of six passengers. The customer is responsible for providing an accurate passenger count at the time of booking.

Superb Executive Transportation may refuse, cancel, or modify service if the passenger count exceeds the vehicle limit or if the passenger count was misrepresented during booking.

4. Rates, Deposits, and Payment

Rates are based on the service requested, route, date, time, vehicle availability, wait time, and any special service requirements. A quoted rate is not final until confirmed by Superb Executive Transportation.

Website pricing, availability, form selections, or display errors do not bind Superb Executive Transportation unless the reservation and final rate are confirmed in writing.

A deposit may be required to reserve service. Unless Superb Executive Transportation states otherwise in writing, deposits are non-refundable once the reservation is confirmed.

Any remaining balance must be paid before service begins unless Superb Executive Transportation approves a different written payment arrangement.

Accepted forms of payment will be provided at the time of booking or confirmation. Superb Executive Transportation may refuse unapproved payment methods and may require cleared payment before service begins.

5. Cancellations

Customer cancellations may result in loss of deposit or other amounts already paid. Superb Executive Transportation reserves vehicle time, driver availability, and scheduling capacity once a booking is confirmed.

No refund is owed for same-day cancellations, late cancellations, no-shows, refusal to comply with these Terms and Conditions, or termination of service caused by passenger conduct.

6. Delays, Flight Changes, and Schedule Conflicts

Customers must notify Superb Executive Transportation as soon as possible of any flight delay, ferry delay, schedule change, pickup change, or service timing issue.

Superb Executive Transportation will make reasonable scheduling efforts when delays occur. However, delayed arrivals are subject to vehicle and driver availability. If a delay conflicts with another confirmed customer booking, Superb Executive Transportation may adjust the pickup time, offer the next available service window, or cancel the affected service without refund when the delay prevents performance of the original reservation.

Additional wait time, extended service time, route changes, or rescheduling may result in additional charges.

7. No-Shows

A customer is considered a no-show when the customer or passenger does not appear at the agreed pickup location within the scheduled pickup window and does not timely communicate with Superb Executive Transportation.

No-shows are non-refundable. Superb Executive Transportation is not required to continue holding vehicle time after a no-show occurs.

8. Pickup and Drop-Off Information

The customer is responsible for providing accurate pickup, drop-off, date, time, flight, ferry, hotel, villa, and contact information. Superb Executive Transportation is not responsible for delays, missed service, added travel time, or failed pickups caused by incorrect or incomplete information provided by the customer.

The customer must remain reachable by phone, text, or email before and during the scheduled service window. Failure to respond to reasonable coordination messages may delay, modify, cancel, or terminate service without refund.

If the customer or passengers are late, unavailable, at the wrong location, or not ready at the scheduled pickup time, additional wait time charges, service modification, cancellation, or no-show rules may apply.

9. Minors

Minors are not permitted to ride unless accompanied by a responsible adult. The accompanying adult is responsible for the conduct, safety, and supervision of all minors in the vehicle.

The customer is responsible for providing and properly using any legally required child restraint, booster seat, or child safety equipment. Superb Executive Transportation does not provide child safety seats unless expressly agreed in writing before the reservation is confirmed.

10. No Pets or Animals

Pets and animals are not permitted in Superb Executive Transportation vehicles. Customers must disclose any animal-related transportation need before booking so Superb Executive Transportation can determine whether service can be provided or whether another transportation arrangement is required.

11. No Smoking, Vaping, Food, Alcohol, or Open Containers

Smoking and vaping are not permitted in the vehicle. Food, alcohol consumption, open containers, spills, or any activity likely to stain, damage, or create odor inside the vehicle are prohibited unless Superb Executive Transportation gives prior written approval and the activity is lawful.

Cleaning, deodorizing, repair, or downtime caused by passenger conduct may result in additional charges.

12. Intoxicated or Unsafe Passengers

Superb Executive Transportation may refuse, suspend, or terminate service when a passenger appears intoxicated, disorderly, threatening, unsafe, abusive, or unable to ride without creating a safety risk, property risk, or service disruption.

Termination of service for passenger conduct is non-refundable.

13. Illegal Substances, Firearms, and Prohibited Items

Illegal substances, unlawful firearms, weapons, hazardous materials, and prohibited items are not permitted in the vehicle. Superb Executive Transportation may refuse or terminate service if a passenger possesses, uses, displays, transports, or attempts to bring any prohibited item into the vehicle.

Superb Executive Transportation may contact law enforcement when required for safety, legal compliance, or protection of passengers, drivers, property, or the public.

14. Passenger Conduct

Passengers must treat the driver, vehicle, and other passengers respectfully. Abusive language, threats, harassment, disorderly conduct, unsafe behavior, interference with the driver, or refusal to follow reasonable driver instructions may result in immediate termination of service without refund.

The driver has authority to make immediate safety and operational decisions during service, including refusing to begin service, modifying service, stopping service, or terminating service when necessary to protect passengers, the driver, the vehicle, property, or the public.

Passengers must comply with seat belt laws, traffic safety requirements, and driver safety instructions. Superb Executive Transportation may refuse or terminate service when a passenger refuses to comply with lawful safety requirements.

15. Vehicle Damage and Cleaning Fees

The customer is responsible for damage, excessive mess, stains, spills, odors, broken equipment, or required cleaning caused by the customer or any passenger in the customer’s party.

Superb Executive Transportation may charge the customer for cleaning, repair, replacement, loss of use, administrative handling, and related costs. Charges may include professional cleaning, deodorizing, detailing, repair invoices, replacement costs, and vehicle downtime when applicable.

Vomit, bodily fluids, biohazard contamination, urine, blood, excessive odor, or any condition requiring special cleaning may result in immediate termination of service and additional cleaning, sanitizing, repair, and downtime charges.

16. Special Assistance and Accessibility Needs

Customers must disclose mobility limitations, accessibility needs, special assistance needs, oversized luggage, medical equipment, or other transportation-related requirements before the reservation is confirmed. Superb Executive Transportation may determine whether the requested service can be safely and properly provided based on the vehicle, route, timing, driver availability, and operational limitations.

17. Route Changes and Extra Stops

The confirmed reservation covers the agreed route, pickup location, drop-off location, date, time, and service type. Extra stops, route changes, extended waiting, added passengers, luggage changes, or service changes may require approval and may result in additional charges.

18. Luggage and Personal Property

Customers are responsible for their luggage, valuables, personal belongings, travel documents, phones, electronics, and other property. Superb Executive Transportation is not responsible for lost, forgotten, damaged, or stolen personal property unless required by law.

Luggage must fit safely within the vehicle without blocking seats, doors, aisles, driver visibility, or safe operation. Excessive, oversized, wet, leaking, unsafe, or misrepresented luggage may require service modification, refusal, cancellation, or additional charges.

Customers should contact Superb Executive Transportation promptly about any item believed to have been left in a vehicle.

19. Weather, Road Conditions, Ferry Operations, and Events Beyond Control

Service may be affected by weather, traffic, road closures, ferry schedules, mechanical issues, public events, emergencies, law enforcement activity, government restrictions, unsafe conditions, or events beyond Superb Executive Transportation’s control.

Superb Executive Transportation may delay, modify, reschedule, or cancel service when necessary for safety or operational reasons.

If the scheduled vehicle becomes unavailable due to mechanical issues, safety concerns, emergency conditions, or operational necessity, Superb Executive Transportation may provide a comparable vehicle, arrange an alternate transportation solution, reschedule the service, or cancel the affected service when no reasonable substitute is available.

20. Refusal or Termination of Service

Superb Executive Transportation reserves the right to refuse or terminate service for safety concerns, passenger misconduct, prohibited items, inaccurate booking information, excess passengers, unpaid balances, unreasonable risk, or violation of these Terms and Conditions.

Any violation of these Terms and Conditions may result in immediate termination of service without refund.

21. Limitation of Liability

Superb Executive Transportation is not liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages, including missed flights, missed ferries, missed appointments, lost reservations, lost income, or travel disruption, except where liability cannot be limited by law.

Superb Executive Transportation’s responsibility, if any, is limited to the amount paid for the affected service unless a different limit is required by law.

22. Customer Agreement

By submitting a booking request, confirming a reservation, paying a deposit, making payment, entering the vehicle, or using the service, the customer agrees to these Terms and Conditions on behalf of the customer and all passengers in the customer’s party.

A customer who books service for another person or group is responsible for the reservation, payment, communication, passenger information, passenger conduct, and compliance with these Terms and Conditions by all passengers covered by the booking.

23. Payment Disputes and Chargebacks

Customers must contact Superb Executive Transportation directly to resolve any payment question, billing concern, or service dispute. Filing a false, improper, or unsupported chargeback after a confirmed reservation, completed service, no-show, late cancellation, or passenger-conduct termination may result in collection of the unpaid balance, chargeback fees, administrative costs, and other lawful recovery costs.

24. Privacy and Customer Information

Personal information submitted through the website, booking form, email, phone, text message, or other communication is handled according to Superb Executive Transportation’s Privacy Policy.

Superb Executive Transportation will not use a customer’s name, photo, image, trip details, or private booking information for marketing or promotional purposes without permission.

25. Changes to These Terms

Superb Executive Transportation may update these Terms and Conditions at any time. The version posted on this page applies unless Superb Executive Transportation provides different written terms for a specific confirmed reservation.
